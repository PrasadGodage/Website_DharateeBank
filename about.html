Welcome to Dharatee shetkari

Dharatee shetkari was established with a singular vision in mind - to streamline and ensure the smoothest and most dependable trade in the realm of dry fruits, seeds, and dried vegetables. Through our extensive international network and a team of seasoned experts, we have transformed this vision into reality,consistently delivering to you the highest caliber of natural products.
Our founding principle revolves around making your experience in the dry fruits, seeds, and dried vegetables industry as effortless as possible. We understand that in a world where reliability and quality are paramount, it's essential to have a partner you can trust implicitly. That's precisely the role we've carved out for ourselves at Dharatee shetkari - to be your unwavering ally in sourcing, importing, and exporting these natural commodities.
